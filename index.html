"K"?

K is short for Korean. So, Kpop stands for Korean popular music, and Kdrama refers to a Korean TV show.

"Kutz"?

.svg files are files that work with commercial and personal cutting machines, like the Silhouette Cameo, but most people call them cut files. See how we put a "k" for Korea instead of a "c"--cute, eh?

About us

We sell .svg downloads of funny K-related memes. Once you've purchased a file, you can use it as many times as you want. Add cut heat-transfer material to your favorite t-shirt or cut vinyl and add it to a canvas for a friend. The possibilities are endless.

Because we know not everyone has the time to make their own K-creations, you can also choose a base product (t-shirt, mug, or tote) and we'll be happy put the kut of your choice on it.
